### ✨ Hello and welcome!
My name is Vladyslav, I'm a student at Lviv Polytechnic National University. I work in the field of .NET projects and improve my skills in cross-platform development.
### 🥇 My achievements
Now I'm the Team Leader of the [miu-miu enjoyers](https://github.com/miu-miu-enjoyers) team who are also students of my university. We wrote [excemath](https://github.com/miu-miu-enjoyers/excemath) (and [excemath API](https://github.com/miu-miu-enjoyers/excemath-api)) app that helps Ukrainian students to prepare for the exams. The jury, consisting of experienced programmers and analysts, highly rated our application!

If we're talking about my personal projects, I developed [Coin Manager](https://github.com/imlystyi/coin-manager) - the application to manage and watch cryptocurrency market - and [QuickPlans](https://github.com/imlystyi/quickplans) - simple, quick and concise note editor for Android.
